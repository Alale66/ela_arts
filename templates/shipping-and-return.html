Shipping & Delivery

At Ela Arts, we strive to ensure you receive your desired products as quickly as possible. Shipping costs are the customer's responsibility, except for certain items where we offer free shipping. Please check our product pages to see if your item qualifies.

For any shipping inquiries, feel free to contact us. We offer various shipping options to accommodate different needs and budgets.

Return & Refund Policy

At Ela Arts, we take pride in the quality and craftsmanship of our products. If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with your purchase, please review our return policy.

  • Books: Can be returned within 5 days of purchase, provided they are undamaged
  • Gift bags and cards: Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns for these items
  • Digital products: All sales are final for digital downloads

We are here to assist you with your returns. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable and return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us. Thank you for choosing Ela Arts for your book, card, and gift shop needs.
